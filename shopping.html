3 Popular Shopping Malls

1. Fisketorvet Copenhagen Mall


Nothing feels better then roaming around in a huge shopping center with an Imax theatre inside. A really great shopping experience when you have all the big brands under one roof. Lot of food and eating choices as well so you wont be hungry when you are shopping. You can go there with some friends and had great time shopping here. The central fish structure looks magnificent and lot of space at each level to sit in between shopping.

Address: Kalvebod Brygge 59, 1560 København, Denmark

Operating Hours:
  • Sunday to Saturday, 10:00 a.m. - 8:00 p.m.
Phone Number: +45 33 36 64 00

2. Field's


It is a big decent shopping mall with variety of stores. A big grocery store on the ground level. Food court has some renovation work going on, but there are still plenty of good restaurants to choose from. Most of them offer buffees.

Address: 12, Arne Jacobsens Allé, 2300 København, Denmark

Operating Hours:
  • Sunday to Saturday, 10:00 a.m. - 8:00 p.m.
Phone Number: +45 70 20 85 05

3. Bryggen


The department store looks architecturally beautiful both outside and inside. It's a nice shopping mall. In the inside there ots of shops from food store to clothing shops. There are few nice restaurant inside and the inside is very clean and parking is available in the building.

Address: Søndertorv 2, 7100 Vejle, Denmark

Operating Hours:
  • Sunday to Saturday, 10:00 a.m. - 7:00 p.m.
Phone Number: +45 70 10 55 60
