Foods and beverages

Foods


  1. Smørrebrød/smør og brød

    2. Pronounciation:

    Smørrebrød [ˈsmɶɐ̯ˌpʁœðˀ], also name as 'butter and bread' in English is a traditional open-faced sandwich in the cuisines of Denmark.

    The ingredients of Smørrebrød:

    • Rugbrød (a dense, dark brown bread)
    • Cold cuts
    • Meat/fish
    • Thinly sliced cheese
    • Cucumber/tomatoes/red onions
    • Boiled eggs

  2. Aebleflæsk

    3. Pronounciation:

    Aebleflæsk [ˈeːpləˌflesk] is a traditional Danish dish that consisted of cured or salted pork belly/flæsk fried with apple chopped onion mix, thyme and sugar.

      Steps on making Aebleflæsk:
    1. Pork belly is fried first in skillet.
    2. Fried pork belly is moved to a plate, tart apple is added into skillet.
    3. Add spices and sugar into the skillet, mix together.
    4. When the apple is soften, serve it on the plate.
    5. Mixed it with rugbrød and enjoy it!

    P.S.: It's best to enjoy Aebleflæsk with beer and snaps!
