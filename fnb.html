Smørrebrød/smør og brød

Pronounciation:

Smørrebrød [ˈsmɶɐ̯ˌpʁœðˀ], also name as 'butter and bread' in English is a traditional open-faced sandwich in the cuisines of Denmark.

The ingredients of Smørrebrød:

  • Rugbrød (a dense, dark brown bread)
  • Cold cuts
  • Meat/fish
  • Thinly sliced cheese
  • Cucumber/tomatoes/red onions
  • Boiled eggs
